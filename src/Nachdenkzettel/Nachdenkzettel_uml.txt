4. Was ist der Unterschied zwischen Aggregation und Komposition? Was bedeutet der Unterschied in einer Datenbank?

-Aggregation is a connection that describes the relation between certain objects 
	e.g. classroom and students (the objects are related to each other but are not dependent 
	from one another)
-Composition is a part of aggregation that describes the ownership 
	e.g. destruction of school —> destruction of classroom  

 -The composition can exist without an aggregate object. If the aggregate object is deleted, that does not mean that the composition object is deleted as well.

6. Was bedeutet es, wenn eine Klasse in UML ein „public“ Attribut hat?

-Public means that all classes have access to the specific feature. Access to this feature can only be available if at least one other object is accessible as well.
